"They are hungry, parentless, alone, scared, homeless and without hope..."

We are grateful that our country Malaysia is blessed with prosperous life in majority. Do all of us know how the orphans in other countries especially in India live their life? How many of the spoiled children nowadays crying asking for their parents to buy them toys or expensive gadgets when the truth is there are children in India collect and sell garbage just to buy food in order to survive?

Here I put a heart touching video about life of orphans in India. This is the true story of the orphans living in hardships in India. I truly hope this video can give awareness to each one of us and inspiration for us to care for the orphans even more.
