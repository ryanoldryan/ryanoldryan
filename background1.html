Background

The history of Lembaga Kebajikan Anak-Anak Yatim Sarawak (PERYATIM) started when Pertubuhan Kebajikan Anak-Anak Yatim Negeri Sarawak (PKAYNS) was registered on 3 May 1978. The name had been changed in the year of 2005 and replaced by the establishment of a charitable trust organization which is Lembaga Kebajikan Anak-Anak Yatim Sarawak under The Charitable Trusts Ordinance, Cap. 1994 and was successfully enforced on 1 July 2005, or better known as PERYATIM. The first patron of the PERYATIM is Allahyarhamah Datuk Amar Puan Sri Hajjah Laila Taib with the main purpose of the establishment of the complex was to care for the welfare of orphans throughout the State.

Chronological history of the opening of the four (4) Complex PERYATIM in Sarawak Branch are as follows:
1) Kompleks Kebajikan Laila Taib (KKLT), Kuching Officially launched on June 24, 1993
2) Kompleks Kebajikan Hamidah Yakup (KKHY), Miri Launched on 28 May 2008
3) Kompleks Kebajikan Anak Yatim Sibu Started in June 2014
4) Kompleks Kebajikan Anak Yatim Bintulu Started in March 2015

Residents of the complex orphans comprising various descendants of tribes in Sarawak such as Malay, Melanau, Iban, Bidayuh, Chinese and others. They are between the ages of seven (7) to nineteen (19) years old, from Primary 1 to Form 6.

The orphans need our love and help. Cherish them unconditionally for as long as we live.
