This webpage was created and written by me, Ryanold S.R. Rosidi. This website is about Lembaga Kebajikan Anak-Anak Yatim Sarawak (PERYATIM), a home to the orphans of Sarawak. I believe that all of us do care but sometimes forgot about the orphans and how they actually living their life. For that, I truly hope that this website can give awareness to everyone about the orphans of PERYATIM and the orphans within Malaysia as well as throughout the world.

Of the nearly 140 million children classified as orphans, 15.1 million have lost both parents. Evidence clearly shows that the vast majority of orphans are living with a surviving parent, grandparent, or other family member. 95 per cent of all orphans are over the age of five.

Another note of interest:
The estimated number of total orphans increased from 1990-2001 where it peaked at 155.4 million. Since 2001, the estimated number of total orphans has declined consistently but very slowly – at a rate of only 0.7% per year during this period

• 1990: 146 million

• 1995: 151 million

• 2000: 155 million

• 2005: 153 million

• 2010: 146 million

• 2015: 140 million

( Source of information: Unicef.org )
